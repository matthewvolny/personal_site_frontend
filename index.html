About
In 2021 I ended an 8 year adventure as a Senior Engineer at Google. Since then, I've been working with companies on creative projects, prototyping, or advising their engineering teams. Some of the bigger companies I've worked with (that I can talk about) include Google, Revel and Moderna.Before that, I used to work on Chrome. Do you know that little profile switcher button that in top right corner of your browser? Yeah, I did that (but now it looks different. It's been 6+ years after all). I wrote a thesis on Reinforcement Learning, which you can read here, though I'm not sure why you would. I've recently started focusing less on tech and more on art, which is something I've wanted to do all my life! You can see how that is going on my studio page.
